## 介绍

自从洪荒中突然出现了ChatGPT，这个世界对于码农们来说，似乎变得不一样了。

本来我一个函数可以用一个星期来构思，设计，实现和调试，但是现在，我只需要几句话，就能完成所有的东西。(当然还用一般时间上班摸鱼)

就是我现在坐在电脑前面，写这些文字，AI的自动完成都不停的在给我自动补全句子。
我得不停的删除，才能让这些文字符合我的本意。<--（这句话都是自动补全的）

自从这一切发生，我在享受AI提供的强大效能的同时，脑海中也不停的警铃--这一条路终将带我们去往何方？

各路大神都有各自的说法，但我，没有看到一个答案。我想在现在也没有人能我们一个答案。
既然如此，我辈不能坐以待毙，还是想想怎么在AI时代活下去吧。

The Man Behind the AI Agent

## Introduction

Since the sudden emergence of ChatGPT, the world seems to have become different for code farmers.

Previously, I could spend a whole week conceptualizing, designing, implementing, and debugging a single function; but now, all it takes is a few sentences to get everything done (and of course, spending half the time slacking off at work).

Even right now, as I sit in front of the computer writing these words, AI autocompletion keeps suggesting sentences for me. I have to constantly delete them to make sure these words actually match my original intent. <-- (This sentence was also autocompleted)

Ever since all this happened, while enjoying the immense productivity AI provides, alarm bells have been constantly ringing in my head—where will this path ultimately lead us?

Experts of all kinds have their own opinions, but I haven't seen a single definitive answer. I don't think anyone can give us one right now. Since that is the case, we cannot sit idly by awaiting our doom. We'd better figure out how to survive in the age of AI.
